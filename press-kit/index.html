What's the game all about?

My Nine Lives is a non-violent survival game about cats for cats, though not for them to play it but to raise money to help the stray cats and make their nine lives better, hence 100% of the revenue will be donated to cat charities. The secondary goal of the project is to generate awareness about stray cats and the ways people can help them.

Unique colorful style

Though the theme of the project is serious, the game itself should be fun, so the aesthetics is bright and colourful and aims to give you an optimistic feel.

Low Poly models match with cartoon skyboxes and slight usage of post processing effects like Depth-of-Field or Chromatic Aberration gives the game a final touch.

Infinite procedural world

World in My Nine Lives is randomly generated on-the-fly.

With highly optimized algorithms, it takes just a split of a second to create a new block, which could be situated in a deciduous or coniferous forest, in different season and day time, but overall always unique.

Original and fun gameplay

In the wild, cats are hunters, but also prey. In My Nine Lives you ought to survive by catching your lunch, but stay away from predators.

Sharp cat senses help you analyze the world around you and when you jump it seems like the time suddenly goes slower (feature known as bullet-time). And if you're lucky, you can also meet another cat. Meow! üò∫
